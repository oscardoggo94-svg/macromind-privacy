# Privacy Policy for Macromind

**Last updated:** January 2026

Macromind is a macro and nutrition tracking application designed to help users
track nutrition and fitness goals. Your privacy is important to us.

---

## Information We Collect
Macromind does **not collect, transmit, or store personal data on external
servers**.

---

## Local-Only Data Storage
All app data remains stored locally on the user’s device.

Macromind does **not**:
- Upload data to the internet
- Maintain user databases
- Access user information remotely

Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

---

## Data Sharing
Macromind does **not sell, share, or disclose** personal data to third parties.

---

## Children’s Privacy
Macromind is not intended for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly
collect personal information from children.

---

## Your Choices
You may delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the application.

---

## Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be posted on
this page.
